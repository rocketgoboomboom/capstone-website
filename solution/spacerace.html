Space Race

The purpose of the game is to achieve the greatest possible distance while effectively troubleshooting various errors that arise on your mission. The game involves using data visualizations to monitor spacecraft mechanics such as thrust and cabin pressure. Based on these levels, participants manipulate an Arduino board to get the spacecraft back to stasis.

Space Race is a living example of tasks created from M.A.S.T.
